Rice Recipe

Description

Asian or not, you must know how make make the perfect rice. Not that soft, not that stiff. Mix it with the right curry and your tummy will be full in no time.

Ingredients

  • Rice (obviously)
  • Water

Steps

  1. The first step that most of us get wrong is to wash the rice properly and few times!
  2. After washing the rice, get a cooker and fill that up with water. Put the rice into that cooker.
  3. Check the water level by putting your fingers in the water straight in the water and touch the rice. If the water covers the height of your finger then it is perfect else you need to add more water.
  4. Heat up the water.
  5. When the water starts boiling start the ultimate task of checking the rices softness a couple of times
  6. Keep checking the rice until it gets soft.
  7. There should be excess water left throw it away.
  8. Heat the rice up for 1-2 mins more.
  9. When you start hearing some crackling sounds turn off the heat.
  10. Your rice is ready to be served with curry or whatever your heart or should I say belly desires.
