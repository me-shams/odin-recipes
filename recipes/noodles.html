Noodles Recipe

Description

Stop being hungry and learn how to make noodles as simple as possible.

Ingredients

  • Any instant noodles
  • Oil
  • Onions and green chillies
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Take some water in a pot and start boiling it
  2. When the water starts boiling, put the instant noodles into the water and boil it for 2-3 minutes.
  3. After boiling the noodles, take the noodles out of the hot water and run cold water onto it.
  4. Now take a pan and start frying some onions and green chillies with oil..
  5. After 1 minute of frying them, put egg in it and fry it with some taste maker.
  6. Put the noodles it the pan and cook it for a while. You can add vegetables if you want too.
  7. Noodles are ready! Start eating them.
